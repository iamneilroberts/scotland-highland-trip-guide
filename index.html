Scotland Highland Heritage Trip

Daily Guide for Neil Roberts & Party - April 20-29, 2025

Day 1: Sunday, April 20 Travel Day
Morning/Afternoon: Depart Mobile (MOB)
Evening: Flight to Charlotte (CLT) → London Heathrow (LHR)
Overnight: In transit
Day 2: Monday, April 21 Arrival in Scotland
Morning: Arrive London Heathrow → Flight to Inverness (INV)
Afternoon:
  • Collect rental car at Inverness Airport
  • Drive to Inverness (25 min)
  • Check in at 20a, By The Ness accommodation
Evening: Explore Inverness city center, dinner by River Ness
Accommodation: 20a, By The Ness
Tip: Rest well after travel; short walk along River Ness recommended
Day 3: Tuesday, April 22 Inverness & Loch Ness
Morning: Breakfast at accommodation, explore Inverness Castle viewpoint
Afternoon:
  • Drive to Loch Ness (30 min)
  • Optional Loch Ness boat cruise or Urquhart Castle visit
Evening: Return to Inverness, dinner in city
Accommodation: 20a, By The Ness
Day 4: Wednesday, April 23 Culloden & Clava Cairns
Morning: Breakfast, then visit Culloden Battlefield (15 min drive)
  • Allow 2-3 hours for visitor center and battlefield walk
Afternoon:
  • Clava Cairns prehistoric site (5 min drive from Culloden)
  • Traditional Scottish lunch at a local pub
Evening: Return to Inverness
Accommodation: 20a, By The Ness
Day 5: Thursday, April 24 Highland Exploration
Morning: Breakfast, then scenic drive through eastern Highlands
Afternoon:
  • Visit Highland distillery (options: Glen Ord, Tomatin, or Dalwhinnie)
  • Explore Highland villages
Evening: Return to Inverness for final night
Accommodation: 20a, By The Ness
Day 6: Friday, April 25 Journey to Isle of Skye
Morning:
  • Check out from Inverness accommodation
  • Scenic drive to Isle of Skye (2.5 hours)
  • Cross Skye Bridge
Afternoon:
  • Arrive at Isle of Skye
  • Visit Kilt Rock and Mealt Falls viewpoint
Evening:
  • Check in at Aurora B&B
  • Dinner in Portree
Accommodation: Aurora B&B
Tip: Stop at Eilean Donan Castle en route to Skye
Day 7: Saturday, April 26 Isle of Skye & Raasay Day Trip
Morning:
  • Early breakfast
  • Drive to Sconser (35-40 min)
  • 9:30 AM ferry to Isle of Raasay (25 min journey)
Midday:
  • Visit Isle of Raasay Distillery
  • Lunch at Raasay House or distillery café
Afternoon:
  • Short scenic walk on Raasay (Temptation Hill or Miners' Trail)
  • 3:15 or 4:30 PM ferry back to Skye
Evening: Return to accommodation, dinner in Portree
Accommodation: Aurora B&B
Tip: No advance booking needed for ferry; arrive 15 min early
Day 8: Sunday, April 27 Return to Mainland
Morning:
  • Check out from Skye accommodation
  • Visit any final Skye attractions (Fairy Pools or Old Man of Storr)
Afternoon:
  • Drive east across Skye Bridge to mainland Scotland
  • Scenic route to Inverness/Nairn area
Evening: Free time
Accommodation: TBD
Day 9: Monday, April 28 Nairn Day
Morning: Explore Nairn Beach and harbor
Afternoon:
  • Visit nearby Cawdor Castle
  • Optional golf at championship course
Evening:
  • Check in at Golf View Hotel & Spa
  • Enjoy hotel spa facilities and AA Rosette restaurant
Accommodation: Golf View Hotel & Spa
Tip: Book spa treatments in advance if desired
Day 10: Tuesday, April 29 Return Travel Day
Morning:
  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Check out
  • Drive to Inverness Airport (11 min)
  • Return rental car
Afternoon/Evening:
  • Flight: Inverness (INV) → London Heathrow (LHR)
  • Flight: London Heathrow (LHR) → Charlotte (CLT)
  • Flight: Charlotte (CLT) → Mobile (MOB)

  • April in Scotland: Temperatures range from 5-13°C (40-55°F)
  • Pack layers, waterproof jacket, comfortable walking shoes
  • Portable power bank/adapter for UK outlets (3-pin, 230V)
